About

JalViks - Climate Service

JalViks is a climate-service platform built to support farmers, FPOs and agri-businesses in water-stressed regions. By combining satellite rainfall, ground sensors and advanced AI models, JalViks delivers field-level forecasts of rainfall, temperature and soil moisture, along with simple advisories on when to irrigate, when to sow and how to prepare for extreme events. Our goal is to make every decision on the farm climate-smart—reducing risk, improving yields and making every drop of water count.

Why Choose Us

Where Innovation meets Agriculture

Crop Disease Detection

Get early warnings for pest and disease risk based on weather patterns, crop stage and humidity. JalViks alerts you when conditions favour outbreaks so you can act early and protect your fields.

Autonomous Climate System

An always-on intelligence engine that ingests satellite, station and sensor data to generate plot-wise forecasts, irrigation plans and risk alerts—automatically, every day, without manual effort.

Temperature Monitoring

Track daily and hourly temperature trends for each farm, understand heat and cold stress on your crops, and plan irrigation and field operations with clear, easy-to-read dashboards.

Soil Moisture Monitoring

See how wet or dry each plot is using a combination of in-field sensors and satellite-based soil-moisture maps. Optimise irrigation schedules, avoid both waterlogging and drought stress, and save water without hurting yield.
